Name of the Snack-Bread Sandwich

Recipe of the Bread Sandwich

1.Take two slices of bread.
2.Few peices of tomato,onion and cucumber.
3.Aplly sauce on one slice of bread and green chutney one another slice.
4.Place peices of tomato,onion and cucumber on one slice and cover it with another slice.
5.If you want then you can roast you sandwich on cooking pan.
6.Your sandwich is ready. Taste it you will fell yummy!
